Privacy Policy — PerkVault
Effective Date: April 8, 2026
Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Overview
PerkVault ("the App") is a personal credit card benefits tracking application developed by Stonham Studios LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use PerkVault on your iOS or macOS device.
We are committed to protecting your privacy. PerkVault is designed with a local-first approach — the vast majority of your data never leaves your device.

Information We Collect
Data You Provide — Stored Locally On Your Device
All personal and financial information you enter into PerkVault is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

Credit and charge card names, networks, and annual fees
Card benefit details, dollar values, and usage amounts
Rewards balances and redemption goals
Renewal dates and card-specific notes
Home airport preference
App settings and theme preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers or backend infrastructure. Your financial data remains entirely on your device at all times.
Analytics Data — Mixpanel
PerkVault uses Mixpanel to collect anonymous usage analytics to help us understand how users interact with the app and improve its features. The following data may be collected and sent to Mixpanel:

Screen views and feature interactions
Session length and frequency
Device type, operating system version, and app version
General usage patterns (e.g. which features are used most)

No personally identifiable information is sent to Mixpanel. Analytics data is not linked to your identity and cannot be used to identify you personally. This data is subject to Mixpanel's Privacy Policy.
You can opt out of Mixpanel analytics tracking at any time in the App's Settings screen.
Data Sent to Third-Party Services
Seats.aero
When you use the flight and travel redemption features within PerkVault, the following information is sent to Seats.aero, a third-party award availability search service:

Your home airport code (e.g., "ORD", "LAX")
Travel dates and destination airport codes you search for

This data is sent solely for the purpose of retrieving award seat availability and is subject to Seats.aero's Privacy Policy. We do not control how Seats.aero handles this data.
Subscription Data
If you subscribe to PerkVault Pro, your subscription is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Apple handles all billing and provides us only with anonymized confirmation of your subscription status. Apple's data practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
Advertising (Future)
We may in the future display advertisements to free-tier users through Google AdMob or similar ad networks. If and when this is implemented, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect what data those networks may collect. PerkVault Pro subscribers will not see advertisements.

How We Use Your Information
The information stored on your device is used solely to provide the features of the App. Anonymous analytics data collected via Mixpanel is used solely to:

Understand which features are most useful to users
Identify and fix bugs and performance issues
Prioritize future feature development

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising targeting, or any purpose beyond operating and improving the App.

Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party, except as described below:

Mixpanel — anonymous usage analytics as described above
Seats.aero — airport and travel search data as described above
Apple Inc. — subscription and purchase data processed through the App Store
Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests


Data Retention and Deletion
All personal and financial data stored in PerkVault lives on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the PerkVault app from your device. We do not retain copies of your personal data on any external server.
Anonymous analytics data collected by Mixpanel is retained according to Mixpanel's data retention policies. You may opt out of analytics collection at any time in the App's Settings screen.
To request information about your data or to raise a privacy concern, contact us at the email address below.

Your Privacy Choices

Analytics opt-out — You can disable Mixpanel analytics collection in PerkVault's Settings screen at any time
Data deletion — Delete the app to remove all locally stored data from your device


Children's Privacy
PerkVault is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Security
Because your personal and financial data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including Face ID, Touch ID, and device encryption. We encourage you to use a strong passcode and keep your device's operating system up to date.
